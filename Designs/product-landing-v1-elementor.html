Who Should Take This Course?

This course is designed for workers involved in hazardous waste operations, emergency responders, and site safety supervisors. Whether you're fulfilling a compliance requirement or building foundational safety knowledge, this 40-hour course meets OSHA 29 CFR 1910.120 standards.

Upon completion, participants will be equipped to safely navigate hazardous environments, select appropriate PPE, and execute emergency response protocols with confidence.

Course Outline

Module 1: Introduction to HAZWOPER — Regulations, scope, and your rights as a worker.

Module 2: Hazardous Waste Site Operations — Site characterization, work zones, and controls.

Module 3: Personal Protective Equipment — Selection, donning/doffing, and limitations.

Module 4: Air Monitoring and Sampling — Equipment types and interpretation of results.

Module 5: Decontamination — Procedures for personnel, equipment, and vehicles.

Module 6: Emergency Response and Spill Control — Incident command and emergency action plans.

Module 7: Medical Surveillance — Requirements under 1910.120(f).

Final Exam: Comprehensive assessment covering all modules.
